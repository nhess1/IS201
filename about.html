American Fork High School -> Brigham Young University Education

I was born and raised in Highland Utah and began attending American Fork High School my sophomore year. I participated in student government, track, and cross country, while still excelling in the classroom. I graduated highschool with a 4.0 GPA and an ACT score of 35. I'm currently a Pre-Finance student at Brigham Young University and have maintained a 4.0 GPA through my first two semesters here.

Where the Rubber Meets the Road Work Experience
