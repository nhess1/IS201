Teacher of the Year Timo Mostert

Timo Mostert was born and raised in Indianapolis, Indiana. An ever clever child, Timo excelled in school but struggled to make friends. What did he turn to? Running. Timo began running home from school every day to make it in time for his favorite cartoon. In high school, Timo continued running and that has been a large part of his life ever since. Timo majored in Math and got his degree in teaching. He now teaches statistics and coaches boys cross country and track at American Fork High School. His recent 2012 boys team won the 5A State Championship.

American Fork Cross Country team 2012 State Champions

The boys this year were led by senior Zac Jaclin to a State Championship. They're headed to Arizona in a few weeks to try and claim the Nike Southwest Regional title.

Man behind the scenes... Coach Mostert's Extracurricular

When not coaching, teaching, or grading papers, Mr. Mostert can be found serving in his church calling as a stake high councilor or practicing with his band. Mostert plays the electric guitar like a madman, and if you catch him listening to music you're likely to see him strumming his air guitar. Here he is pictured with his band.
